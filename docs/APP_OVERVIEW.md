VOiD applies a mathematically calculated "adversarial perturbation" to your photos.

- **To Human Eyes:** The photo looks perfect. High quality, sharp, unchanged.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this make my photo look blurry?**
No. The changes are "imperceptible perturbations". They are tiny changes to specific pixels that humans ignore but computers rely on. Your photo remains sharp and beautiful.

**Can I use this for existing photos?**
Yes. You can import any photo from your gallery into VOiD to "scrub" it before posting online.

**Is this legal?**
Absolutely. You have the right to privacy and the right to modify your own creative works (your photos) to protect your biometric data.
